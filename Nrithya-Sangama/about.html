About Us

      Welcome to Nritya Sangama, where we immerse you in the enchanting world of Bharatanatyam, a classical Indian dance form that's steeped in tradition, expression, and grace. We are passionate about preserving and promoting this ancient art, and we invite you to explore its mesmerizing beauty with us.

OUR JOURNEY
Founded 2011, Nritya Sangama has been dedicated to the preservation and propagation of Bharatanatyam for over 15 years. What began as a humble endeavor by Snehalatha Prakash, a visionary artist and dedicated teacher, has now blossomed into a vibrant institution renowned for its commitment to this timeless dance form.

OUR MISSION
At Nritya Sangama, our mission is to share the magic of Bharatanatyam with the world. We strive to provide a nurturing environment where both novices and seasoned dancers can explore their passion for this art. We believe that Bharatanatyam transcends boundaries and connects people through its universal language.

"Bharatanatyam is not just about dance; it's about expressing the divine within."
