Profile

Smt.Vidushi Snehalatha Prakash

Smt.Vidushi Snehalathe Prakash, is a passionate dedicated and a Bharatanatyanm dancer, highly Self-motivated, creative and a firm believer in maintaining the integrity of tradition. The is the founder and director of “Nrithya Sangama” and also certified examiner Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board.

She has performed in several prestigious stage concerts across the state along with her students. Her choreographic works are mesmerizing, which creates magic through derive dancing and contributes towards richness our ancient culture around the world, without compromising classicism of the dance form.







"Bharatanatyam is not just about dance; it's about expressing the divine within."
